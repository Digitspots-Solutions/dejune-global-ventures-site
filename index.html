About Us

DeJune Global Ventures is a leading manufacturing company based in Ado-Odo Ota, Ogun State. We specialize in delivering high-quality manufacturing solutions that meet international standards while understanding the unique needs of the Nigerian market. Our state-of-the-art facility in Ayetoro combines modern technology with skilled craftsmanship to produce products that businesses and industries across Nigeria depend on. With a commitment to excellence, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a trusted manufacturing partner for companies seeking reliability and quality.
